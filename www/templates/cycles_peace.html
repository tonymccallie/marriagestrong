Discover Your Peace Cycle

To break the pain cycle you’ll need to connect to what’s true about you. The sources for these truths come from 3 places:

• Other people (meaningful/close relationships in your life)
• Spiritual resources, and
• The messages you believe about yourself.

In creating your Peace Cycle choose truths that replace the feelings/negative messages in your pain cycle. The truth about me is I am (choose three):

  • Loved
  • Known
  • Accepted
  • Able to Control Self
  • Empowered
  • Appreciated
  • Encouraged
  • Priceless
  • Full of Worth
  • Full of Promise
  • Wanted
  • Valuable
  • Adequate
  • Connected
  • Valued
  • Treasured
  • Celebrated
  • Significant
  • Safe

 REFLECT A SEC:

How do you know the words you have selected are true? Can you confirm the truths through your relationship with God or with others? Most importantly, can you claim them for yourself?
