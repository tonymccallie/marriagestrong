(RING ICON) Spiritual Gifts

For couples interested in their spiritual Us-ness, you can take a spiritual gift inventory online; there are a lot available from different theological perspectives. If you are more familiar with spiritual gifts, choose your top three from the list below:

  • TEACHING
    Communicates Information Well
  • WISDOM
    Deep Insight Into Application Of Knowledge
  • KNOWLEDGE
    Loves Information
  • EXHORTATION
    Comforting And Encouraging
  • DISCERNMENT
    Easily Sees Truth
  • GIVING
    Joyfully Contributes Time And Money
  • MERCY
    Great Compassion
  • EVANGELISM
    Telling Others About Christ
  • HOSPITALITY
    Provides A Warm Welcome
  • FAITH
    Extraordinary Confidence In God
  • LEADERSHIP
    Sets And Achieves Goals For Groups With Ease
  • ADMINISTRATION
    Creates And Executes Effective Plans
  • HEALING
    Restores Health
  • TONGUES
    Speaking In Another Language
  • INTERCESSION
    Loves To Pray
  • SERVICE
    Identifies Need And Completes Tasks

All the above gifts are found in I Corinthians 12, Romans 12 and Ephesians 4. Other spiritual gifts tests include gifts not listed above. Feel free to add to the list:
