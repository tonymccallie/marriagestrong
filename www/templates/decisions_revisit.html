REVISIT PAST DECISIONS

There are times when decisions you make need to change or adjust. Just when you and your spouse have settled on a budget, the car needs repairs. Just when you figure out how to parent your 2-year-old, she turns 3.

Take a look at your past decisions and make an Us choice on how you feel about them today.

###Decision Sample one

  • GREAT DECISION:
    Keep moving forward.
  • GOOD, BUT NEEDS MODIFICATION:
    Options: have an Us conversation about what adjustments to make. If needed, head back to make the New Decision tool and work through your process again.
  • NEED TO RETHINK OUR DECISION:
    A new Us decision awaits you!
